Japan: Kakigori


Kakigori, or shaved ice, is a traditional summer dessert in Japan. Typically, the ice is unflavored and is topped with syrups - common flavors include matcha, melon, mango, and other fruits. There are a variety of different toppings available, such as condensed milk, sweet azuki beans, mochi, or jellies.

Matcha Kakigori Recipe

Ingredient Amount
Green tea powder (matcha) 1 teaspoon
Hot water 2 tablespoons
Granulated sugar 2 teaspoons
Sweetened condensed milk 2 tablespoons
Red bean paste (anko) 2 tablespoons
Ice block 1

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Dissolve green tea powder and granulated sugar in 2 tablespoons of hot water.
  3. Cool the syrup and put in a small serving bowl.
  4. Shave ice over the green tea syrup, using a home shaved ice machine.
  5. Top with anko.
  6. Pour sweetened condensed milk over the ice.
